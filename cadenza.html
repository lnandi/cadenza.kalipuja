Upcoming Celebrations

Mark Your Calendars and Join Us in the Festivities!

Kali Puja 2025

October 19th (Saturday)

Evening: Cultural programs by residents, music, and dance performances. Inauguration of food stalls.

October 20th (Sunday)

Morning: Alpona and Pandal decoration.

Evening: Commencement of Kali Puja rituals.

Night: Pushpanjali followed by Maha Prasad (Bhog) distribution.

Saraswati Puja 2026

February 1st (Sunday)

Morning: Puja commencement, Pushpanjali, and "Hate Khori" for children.

Afternoon: Prasad distribution.

Evening: Cultural program featuring children's art competition, recitation, and music.
